FAQ

What kind of signals does Scoop detect?

Executive changes, funding rounds, M&A activity, layoffs, product launches, regulatory actions, strategic pivots, and earnings surprises. We run 8 parallel research queries per company and filter to what's relevant to your deals.

How is this different from Google Alerts?

Google Alerts sends you raw links. Scoop gives you a synthesized brief with context on why each signal matters to your specific deal. It includes opening lines, suggested next steps, and risk flags. It's the difference between a news feed and an intelligence analyst.

What if my accounts are small or private companies?

Scoop works best with companies that have some public footprint. For smaller accounts, we surface signals from industry publications, job postings, social media, and regulatory filings that larger tools miss.

Do I need to install anything?

No. Scoop is email-only. No app, no browser extension, no CRM plugin. It shows up in your inbox every Monday morning. Reply to any digest to ask follow-up questions.

Is it really free?

Free forever for up to 10 accounts. No credit card required. Reply "stop" to any digest to unsubscribe instantly.

Can I self-host Scoop?

Yes. Scoop is fully open source (MIT license). Clone the repo, add your API keys, and run it on your own infrastructure. Full setup instructions on GitHub.
